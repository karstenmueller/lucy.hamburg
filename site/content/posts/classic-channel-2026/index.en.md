## What it is about

This race is all about spirited, seamanlike sailing across the English Channel in style and forging new friendships along the way. Most of the yachts and crews are likely to hail from the UK and France, giving the whole event a distinctly Anglo-French flavour. And … **Lucy is one of the pre-registered boats!** Have a look for yourself at [https://www.classic-channel-regatta.eu](https://www.classic-channel-regatta.eu)
